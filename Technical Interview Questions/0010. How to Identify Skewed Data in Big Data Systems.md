## âœ… What Next?

Once you've **identified skew**, you can mitigate it using:

* **Salting the key** (for skewed joins)
* **Broadcast joins** (for small tables)
* **Repartitioning**
* **Using adaptive execution** (`spark.sql.adaptive.skewJoin.enabled=true`)
